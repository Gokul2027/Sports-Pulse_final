Hear from our alumni about their memorable experiences in various sporting events at SRM University.

Rahul Sharma - Football Champion

"The thrill of playing in the finals was indescribable! Winning the championship brought our team together like never before."

During my time at SRM, I had the incredible opportunity to lead our football team to the finals. The support from our coaches and fellow students created an electrifying atmosphere.The thrill of playing in the finals was indescribable! It was the culmination of a season filled with hard work and determination. As we stepped onto the field, the excitement from our fans fueled our adrenaline. The first half was intense, with both teams giving their all. Despite falling behind just before halftime, we regrouped and focused on our strategy. The second half saw us playing with renewed vigor, showcasing our teamwork. With only minutes left and the score tied, I positioned myself near the penalty area. When our midfielder made a perfect cross, I leaped and connected with the ball, scoring the winning goal. The stadium erupted in cheers, and we had done it! Lifting the trophy together was surreal. It symbolized not just victory, but the bonds we formed and the perseverance we demonstrated throughout the season. Those experiences taught me invaluable lessons in leadership and teamwork that I carry into my professional life today. The championship match at SRM University will always remind me of the power of collective effort and the joy of shared success.

Aditya Roy - Basketball MVP

"Being crowned MVP was a dream come true! The teamwork and dedication we put in were the keys to our success."

Winning the state championship as the MVP of the basketball team was one of the highlights of my university life.Being crowned MVP was a dream come true! The journey to that moment was filled with countless hours of practice and unwavering dedication from our entire team. As the season progressed, we faced numerous challenges, including injuries and tough opponents. However, each practice brought us closer together, solidifying our chemistry on and off the court. The final match was a true test of our skills and determination. I vividly remember the adrenaline rush as the clock wound down, and we were neck and neck with our rivals. With just seconds left, I received the ball and made a crucial shot that clinched our victory. The roar of our supporters was deafening, and holding the trophy aloft felt surreal.
